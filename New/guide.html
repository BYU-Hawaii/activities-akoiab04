Gameplay Instructions



1. Clicking Tiles: Click on a numbered tile adjacent to the empty space to move it.

2. Strategy: Plan your moves strategically to achieve the correct order efficiently.

3. Completion: Once all tiles are in order (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, null), a message will appear congratulating you on solving the puzzle.

Tips for Success

- Start with the edges: Begin by moving tiles from the edges towards the center to create space for subsequent moves.

- Visualize: Visualize the end goal (ordered tiles) to plan your moves effectively.

- Patience: Sliding puzzles require patience and persistence. Don't be afraid to experiment with different strategies.

Example Gameplay

1. Start with a shuffled board.

2. Click on tiles adjacent to the empty space to move them.

3. Continue moving tiles until all numbers are in order and the empty space is in the bottom-right corner (null).

4. Upon completing the puzzle, an alert will confirm your success.
